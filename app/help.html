Triangula Leveleditor Help

Steps to a successfull Triangula level:

Preface

1. The player always starts at the left side of the level. The position is indicated by the small white dot.

2. By tapping and holding the player can move back in time. Use this features to make your level awesome as hell!

Step 1: Triangles

Almost everything in Triangula is made out of triangles. Use the left button to add a triangle to the level:

You can also press t which inserts a triangle at your current cursor position

Pro tip

Step 2: Colors

Define your level colors below the level area. The first and fith color define the colors of the triangles. The other three are used to generate the background. Find some really great color palettes here: Adobe Kuler

Step 3: Level Elements

These Elements make Triangula so much fun:

  • Spikes: Some triangles which always move up and down
  • Doors: A switch and a triangle (door). When the player touches the switch, the triangle moves up
  • Bombs: Touch 'em and you loose.
  • Exits: The player must reach any exit to successfully end the level.

You can also press b which inserts a bomb at your current cursor position

Pro tip

Step 4: Give your beauty a name

You have to give your level a name. After you click save it appears in your level history.

If you have a creator tag, you can enter it in the right input field. You can then instantly play the level on your phone / tablet. The level name is always [creator-tag]-[level-name]. For example: your creator tag is "yay" and your level is called "superawesomeness". You then need to enter "yay-superawesomeness" in the level input field in the game.
